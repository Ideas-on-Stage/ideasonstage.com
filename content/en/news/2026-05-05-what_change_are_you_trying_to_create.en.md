Before you start designing slides or rehearsing lines, ask yourself: *What change am I trying to create*?

Do you want your audience to see something differently? Do something they hadn’t planned? Feel something more deeply?

A good presentation leads your audience somewhere new.

Every choice you make – what to include, what to leave out – should support that shift.

If the change feels too big for one talk, narrow it. Make it clear and achievable.

The real result of a great talk shows up after it ends.
